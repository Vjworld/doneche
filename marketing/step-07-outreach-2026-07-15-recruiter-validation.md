## Context
New model to validate: **"Reverse Recruiter"** — a B2B angle where doneche flips the ghosting problem around and helps recruiters/agencies look responsive (or gives them visibility into which candidates are being silently ghosted by their own pipeline, and lets doneche act as a channel to re-engage warm candidates on their behalf). This is a **validation-stage cold DM** — the goal is conversation and signal, not a hard pitch. Keep it short, curiosity-driven, low-commitment ask.

Target: Tech recruiters / talent acquisition folks on LinkedIn (agency + in-house).

## Send Notes / Playbook
- **Sequence:** Send Primary template first. If no reply in 4-5 days, follow up once with a one-line bump: *"Totally understand if timing's off — even a one-line reply on whether this is a real problem for you would help a ton."*
- **Target list:** Start with warm/2nd-degree connections (agency recruiters, in-house TA at mid-size tech cos) before cold outreach to 3rd-degree.
- **Goal per convo:** Confirm/deny 3 things — (1) is candidate ghosting-by-recruiter a real pain, (2) would they use a tool to flag it, (3) would they (or their agency) pay for it.
- **Do NOT** pitch pricing or a demo in the first message — this is discovery, not a sales pitch. Keep the ask to "10 mins" only.
- **Log every response** (interested / not a pain / pain but wouldn't pay / referred someone else) — feed this back into a future `/money-strategy` pass before building the Reverse Recruiter feature.
